TechnologyCollaboration
An aggregate of two or more nodes that work together to perform collective technology behavior.

A technology collaboration specifies which nodes cooperate to perform some task.

The collaborative behavior, including, for example, the communication pattern of these nodes, is modeled by a technology interaction.

A technology collaboration typically models a logical or temporary collaboration of nodes, and does not exist as a separate entity in the enterprise.
